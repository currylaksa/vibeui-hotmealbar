03 — Where it comes from

A Silk-Road table,
set in Malaysia.

Hot Meal Bar cooks in the Hui and Xinjiang tradition of northwestern China — wheat pulled into noodles, beef simmered slow, spice handled with respect. Every dish is fully halal, the way it has always been made in our community.

For us, feeding people well is its own kind of dakwah: heritage you can taste, shared one hot bowl at a time.
